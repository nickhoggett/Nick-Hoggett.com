UWL Student Health & Safety Test

While working for the University of West London in the Employment & Placement Services department. The managers were looking for a way in which they could automatically test the students pn health and safet y in the work environment who were due to take a placements and gain work experience, and ensure they knew everything they needed to know.

There turned out to be no easy solution to this requirement as the department could not include any of student details on a third party platform and were looking to setup a test which worked as a questionnaire that could only be submitted back to the department once all questions had been answered correctly.

After having researched and exhuasted all options I had suggested to them that I could write them the front end of a form which incorperated Javascript functions that would meet their requirements should they wish host this on their servers.

This is that script, it's here to show my understanding of frontend Javascipt development and is the first form I've written for a client which relies on Javascript.

Please note this has been posted here with the universities copyrighted content removed and is here to show simply how it functioned as a solution to what they needed.
