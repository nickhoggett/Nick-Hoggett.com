My Top 50 Albums of 2017

2017 was a really good year for music and here is a list of albums that I have listened to and really enjoyed throughout the year. This is my opinion on which were the best records also a little bit of information on each.

50. Dan Deacon - Rat Film (Original Soundtrack)

Experimental musician Dan Deacon has teamed up with movie director Theo Anthony for his essay-film Rat Film, a movie based on the rat current rat investation of their home town Baltimore. The music is composed all electronically with the actual movement of rats and fitted the music perfectally

49. Nick Hakim - Green Twins

Hakim's debut album is a great collection of psychedelic songs taking inspiration from soul singers such as Curtis Mayfield & Marvin Gaye often making all the arrangements very daydreamy followed by the occasional bursts of loud unexpected colour.
